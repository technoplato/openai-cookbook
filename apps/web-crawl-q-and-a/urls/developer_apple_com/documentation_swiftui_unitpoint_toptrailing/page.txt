topTrailing
A point that’s in the top, trailing corner of a view.
iOS 13.0+
iPadOS 13.0+
macOS 10.15+
Mac Catalyst 13.0+
tvOS 13.0+
watchOS 6.0+
visionOS 1.0+ Beta
static let topTrailing: UnitPoint
Discussion
This point occupies the position where the horizontal and vertical alignment guides intersect for topTrailing alignment. The trailing edge appears on the right in a left-to-right language environment and on the left in a right-to-left environment.
