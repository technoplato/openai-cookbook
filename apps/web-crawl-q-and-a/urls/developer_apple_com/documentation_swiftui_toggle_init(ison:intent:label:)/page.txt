init<I>(
    isOn: Bool,
    intent: I,
    @ViewBuilder label: () -> Label

Available when Label conforms to View.
Parameters
isOn
Whether the toggle is on or off.
intent
The AppIntent to be performed.
label
A view that describes the purpose of the toggle.
