ColumnSelectorTransformer
A transformer that applies a base transformer to specific columns in a data frame.
iOS 16.0+
iPadOS 16.0+
macOS 13.0+
Mac Catalyst 16.0+
tvOS 16.0+
visionOS 1.0+ Beta
