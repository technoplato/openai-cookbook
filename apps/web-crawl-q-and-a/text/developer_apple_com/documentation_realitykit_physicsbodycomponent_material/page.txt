material
The physics body’s material properties, like friction.
iOS 13.0+
iPadOS 13.0+
macOS 10.15+
Mac Catalyst 13.0+

var material: PhysicsMaterialResource
Discussion
By default, the body’s material resource is set to default, which provides a modest amount of friction and restitution (bounciness).
